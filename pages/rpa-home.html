Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) has its roots in the early 2000s, emerging as businesses sought to automate repetitive tasks in enterprise applications. By combining technologies like screen scraping and workflow automation, RPA evolved into a powerful tool for improving efficiency and reducing costs.
