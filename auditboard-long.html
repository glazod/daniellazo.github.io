1. Problem Overview

A key component of what Controllership tracks is aged overdue issues, which are reported to the companyâ€™s Chief Accounting Office and CFO monthly. These issues are critical to resolve timely as they can cover anything from lack of internal controls around the revenue recognition process to risks around financial misstatements. While AuditBoard comes with a set of pre-built dashboards, none are capable of tracking aged overdue issues.

In a nutshell, global and regional controllers do not have a seamless way to track the aging of open overdue issues raised from SOX Reviews or Internal Audit engagements. As such, a custom dashboard must be built for visibility into these issues.

2. Stakeholder Expectations

Controllers are able to track overdue aged issues assigned to their segment. It is also possible to view these issues based on assigned impact and aging bucket. Additionally, users can navigate directly from the dashboard to the specific issue in the system with a single click.
