System Overview

SAP Concur T&E (Travel & Expense) is a cloud-based solution that automates and manages business travel and expense processes for organizations. It is widely used to streamline the submission, approval, and auditing of employee travel and expense claims.

Problem Statement

No current mechanism to audit fraud effectively. SAP solution is too expensive.

Expected Outcome

Develop a continuous auditing platform for Travel & Entertainment (T&E) expense data, to allow Internal Audit/ Global Controllers to assess T&E activities, identify trends, flag anomalies, and tag policy exceptions in real time. Create alerts. Develop a powerful tool for detecting fraud, anomalies, and waste in travel and expense activities. Each analytic visual is designed to surface specific risk indicators, enabling auditors to proactively identify and investigate suspicious behaviors and transactions.
